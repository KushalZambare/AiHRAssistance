WORKING HOURS POLICY
====================

Effective Date: January 1, 2025
Applicable To: All full-time and part-time employees

1. STANDARD WORKING HOURS
   - The standard workweek is Monday to Friday.
   - Core working hours are 9:30 AM to 6:30 PM (IST), inclusive of a 1-hour lunch break.
   - Total productive hours expected: 8 hours per day / 40 hours per week.

2. FLEXIBLE WORKING HOURS
   - Employees may opt for flexible start times between 8:00 AM and 10:30 AM.
   - The core overlap window (10:30 AM – 4:30 PM) is mandatory for all employees.
   - Flexi-time must be pre-approved by the reporting manager.

3. OVERTIME
   - Employees working beyond standard hours on weekdays will be compensated with
     compensatory off (comp-off), to be availed within 30 days.
   - Weekend work (Saturday/Sunday) requires prior manager approval and qualifies for
     comp-off or overtime pay as applicable by role and grade.

4. ATTENDANCE TRACKING
   - Attendance is tracked via the biometric system / HR portal.
   - Employees working remotely must mark attendance on the HR portal by 10:00 AM.
   - Three consecutive unnotified absences will trigger an HR review.

5. BREAKS
   - Lunch break: 1 hour (between 12:30 PM and 2:30 PM).
   - Short breaks: Employees are entitled to two 15-minute short breaks during the day.

6. PART-TIME EMPLOYEES
   - Part-time employees work a minimum of 20 hours per week.
   - Schedules are agreed upon individually between the employee and HR.

7. ON-CALL DUTIES
   - Certain roles may require on-call availability outside standard hours.
   - On-call duties will be communicated in the employment contract or role description.

8. PUBLIC HOLIDAY WORKING
   - Employees required to work on declared public holidays will receive either
     double pay or a compensatory day off, as mutually agreed.
