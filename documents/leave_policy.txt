LEAVE POLICY
============

Effective Date: January 1, 2025
Applicable To: All full-time employees

1. ANNUAL / EARNED LEAVE
   - Full-time employees are entitled to 18 days of earned leave per year.
   - Leave accrues at 1.5 days per month starting from the date of joining.
   - A maximum of 30 days can be carried forward to the next calendar year.
   - Unused leave beyond the carry-forward limit will lapse.

2. SICK LEAVE
   - Employees are entitled to 12 days of sick leave per year.
   - Sick leave does not carry forward to the next year.
   - A medical certificate is required for sick leave exceeding 3 consecutive days.

3. CASUAL LEAVE
   - Employees are entitled to 6 days of casual leave per year.
   - Casual leave cannot be carried forward or encashed.
   - Casual leave must be applied at least 1 day in advance (except in emergencies).

4. MATERNITY LEAVE
   - Female employees are entitled to 26 weeks of paid maternity leave for the first two children.
   - For the third child and beyond, maternity leave is 12 weeks.
   - An adoption leave of 12 weeks is available for mothers adopting a child below 3 months of age.

5. PATERNITY LEAVE
   - Male employees are entitled to 5 working days of paid paternity leave.
   - Paternity leave must be availed within 6 months of the birth/adoption of the child.

6. BEREAVEMENT LEAVE
   - Employees are entitled to 3 days of paid bereavement leave for an immediate family member
     (spouse, child, parent, or sibling).

7. LEAVE WITHOUT PAY (LWP)
   - Employees may apply for LWP once all applicable paid leaves are exhausted.
   - LWP must be approved by the reporting manager and HR department.

8. PUBLIC HOLIDAYS
   - The company observes 10 public/national holidays per year as notified annually.

9. LEAVE APPLICATION PROCESS
   - All leave requests must be submitted through the HR portal.
   - Approval from the direct reporting manager is mandatory before availing leave.
   - Emergency leaves must be communicated via phone/email on the same day.
