EMPLOYEE HANDBOOK
==================

Welcome to TechCorp Solutions Pvt. Ltd.!
We are excited to have you as part of our team. This handbook provides an overview of
our company culture, values, policies, and the resources available to you.

1. ABOUT THE COMPANY
   TechCorp Solutions is a product-driven technology company founded in 2015,
   headquartered in Pune, India, with offices in Mumbai and Bengaluru.
   We build enterprise SaaS products serving clients across BFSI, Healthcare, and Retail sectors.

2. OUR VALUES
   - Integrity     : We act with honesty and hold ourselves accountable.
   - Innovation    : We embrace change and encourage creative thinking.
   - Collaboration : We achieve more when we work together.
   - Customer First: Every decision is made with our customers in mind.
   - Growth Mindset: We invest in continuous learning and improvement.

3. CODE OF CONDUCT
   - Treat all colleagues, clients, and vendors with respect and professionalism.
   - Maintain confidentiality of company and client data at all times.
   - Avoid any conflict of interest and disclose potential conflicts to HR.
   - Do not engage in harassment, discrimination, or bullying of any kind.
   - Report unethical behavior to HR or the whistleblower hotline anonymously.

4. ONBOARDING
   - New employees complete a structured onboarding program in their first two weeks.
   - Includes: company introduction, tool access setup, policy orientations, and team introductions.
   - A buddy/mentor will be assigned to each new joiner.

5. PERFORMANCE MANAGEMENT
   - Performance reviews are conducted biannually (June and December).
   - Goal-setting is done at the start of each half-year using the OKR framework.
   - Employees are rated on a 5-point scale: Exceptional, Exceeds Expectations,
     Meets Expectations, Needs Improvement, Unsatisfactory.

6. GRIEVANCE REDRESSAL
   - Employees may raise grievances with their HR Business Partner at any time.
   - A formal grievance resolution process will be initiated within 3 working days.
   - Anonymous reporting is available via the HR portal.

7. DISCIPLINARY POLICY
   - Minor violations: Written warning.
   - Repeated/serious violations: Final warning or suspension.
   - Gross misconduct (fraud, harassment, data theft): Immediate termination.

8. TRAINING & DEVELOPMENT
   - All employees undergo mandatory training in: Information Security, POSH (Prevention of
     Sexual Harassment), and Emergency Safety procedures annually.
   - Role-specific technical training is organized by the L&D team.

9. COMMUNICATION CHANNELS
   - Internal communication: Slack, Microsoft Teams, and company email.
   - Announcements: HR portal and all-hands meetings held monthly.
   - HR queries: hr@techcorpsolutions.com or HR Help Desk (Ext. 1001).

10. IT & DATA SECURITY
    - All employees must sign the Data Confidentiality Agreement on Day 1.
    - Company devices must be password-protected and locked when unattended.
    - Sharing of login credentials is strictly prohibited.
    - Any suspected security breach must be reported to the IT Security team immediately.

We look forward to a rewarding journey together.
— The HR Team, TechCorp Solutions Pvt. Ltd.
