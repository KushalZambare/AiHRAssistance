COMPANY BENEFITS & PERKS
=========================

Effective Date: January 1, 2025
Applicable To: All full-time employees

1. HEALTH INSURANCE
   - Group medical insurance of ₹5,00,000 per employee per year.
   - Coverage extended to spouse, two dependent children, and parents.
   - Cashless hospitalization at 5,000+ network hospitals across India.
   - Annual health check-up (up to ₹3,000) fully reimbursed.

2. TERM LIFE & ACCIDENTAL INSURANCE
   - Group term life insurance: 3× annual CTC.
   - Personal accident cover: 5× annual CTC.

3. PROVIDENT FUND (PF) & GRATUITY
   - PF contribution: 12% of basic salary by both employer and employee.
   - Gratuity paid as per the Payment of Gratuity Act, 1972 (after 5 years of service).

4. PERFORMANCE BONUSES
   - Annual performance bonus of up to 15% of CTC based on individual and company performance.
   - Spot bonuses awarded for exceptional contributions at manager's discretion.

5. LEARNING & DEVELOPMENT
   - Annual learning budget of ₹20,000 per employee for courses, certifications, and books.
   - Access to LinkedIn Learning and Udemy for Business (company-sponsored licenses).
   - Internal mentorship programs and leadership development workshops.

6. EMPLOYEE STOCK OPTIONS (ESOP)
   - Eligible senior employees and high performers may receive ESOPs.
   - Vesting schedule: 4 years with 1-year cliff.

7. FOOD & MEAL BENEFITS
   - Meal cards loaded with ₹2,200 per month (tax-exempt).
   - Subsidized cafeteria available at office premises.

8. TRANSPORTATION
   - Cab facility available for employees working late (after 8:00 PM).
   - Fuel/transport reimbursement of up to ₹1,500 per month for field roles.

9. WELLNESS BENEFITS
   - Annual wellness allowance of ₹5,000 for gym memberships, yoga, or fitness apps.
   - Free Employee Assistance Program (EAP) — 24/7 counseling and mental health support.

10. FLEXIBLE BENEFITS PLAN
    - Employees can customize their CTC structure by opting into allowances such as:
      HRA, LTA, telephone reimbursement, and professional development allowance.

11. REFERRAL BONUS
    - Employees referring a successfully hired candidate receive a referral bonus of:
      - ₹15,000 for junior roles
      - ₹30,000 for mid/senior roles
    - Bonus is paid after the referred employee completes 3 months of employment.

12. PARENTAL SUPPORT
    - Maternity and paternity leave as per leave policy.
    - Day-care reimbursement of up to ₹3,000 per month for employees with children under 5.

13. TEAM OUTINGS & EVENTS
    - Quarterly team outings funded by the company.
    - Annual company offsite for all employees.
    - Festival celebrations, birthdays, and work anniversaries are recognized.
