JOB DESCRIPTIONS
================

1. SOFTWARE ENGINEER (BACKEND)
   ----------------------------------
   Department   : Engineering
   Location     : Hybrid (Pune, India)
   Experience   : 2–5 years

   Responsibilities:
   - Design, develop, and maintain scalable backend services and REST APIs.
   - Collaborate with frontend engineers and product managers on feature delivery.
   - Write clean, testable, and well-documented code.
   - Participate in code reviews and contribute to technical architecture discussions.
   - Troubleshoot and resolve production issues in a timely manner.

   Required Skills:
   - Proficiency in Python and/or Java.
   - Strong understanding of SQL and NoSQL databases (PostgreSQL, MongoDB).
   - Experience with Git version control.
   - Knowledge of RESTful API design principles.
   - Familiarity with Docker and CI/CD pipelines.

   Good to Have:
   - Experience with cloud platforms (AWS, GCP, or Azure).
   - Knowledge of microservices architecture.

2. DATA ANALYST
   ----------------------------------
   Department   : Data & Analytics
   Location     : Remote-friendly
   Experience   : 1–3 years

   Responsibilities:
   - Collect, clean, and analyze large datasets to generate business insights.
   - Build dashboards and reports using BI tools (Power BI, Tableau).
   - Work closely with business stakeholders to understand data requirements.
   - Develop and maintain SQL queries and data pipelines.

   Required Skills:
   - Strong SQL skills.
   - Proficiency in Python (pandas, NumPy) for data manipulation.
   - Experience with data visualization tools.
   - Good communication and storytelling skills with data.

3. HR GENERALIST
   ----------------------------------
   Department   : Human Resources
   Location     : On-site (Mumbai, India)
   Experience   : 2–4 years

   Responsibilities:
   - Manage end-to-end recruitment lifecycle.
   - Handle employee onboarding, induction programs, and documentation.
   - Administer payroll inputs and maintain employee records.
   - Address employee grievances and conduct exit interviews.
   - Ensure compliance with labor laws and company HR policies.

   Required Skills:
   - Solid understanding of HR processes and labor legislation.
   - Proficiency in MS Office and HRMS tools.
   - Strong interpersonal and organizational skills.

4. DEVOPS ENGINEER
   ----------------------------------
   Department   : Infrastructure & DevOps
   Location     : Hybrid
   Experience   : 3–6 years

   Responsibilities:
   - Build and maintain CI/CD pipelines for automated testing and deployment.
   - Manage cloud infrastructure on AWS/GCP using Terraform or equivalent IaC tools.
   - Monitor system health, respond to incidents, and perform root cause analysis.
   - Collaborate with development teams to improve deployment reliability.

   Required Skills:
   - Expertise in Linux/Unix systems administration.
   - Experience with Docker, Kubernetes, and container orchestration.
   - Strong scripting skills (Bash, Python).
   - Experience with Git and GitOps practices.
